Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how sortmypay.co.uk ("sortmypay", "we", "us") handles information when you use our website and calculators. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping things simple and transparent.

The short version

Our calculators run entirely in your web browser. When you type figures into a calculator — your salary, dates, dividends and so on — those figures are processed on your own device to show you a result. They are not sent to us, and we do not store them. We do not require you to create an account or sign in.

Information we collect

We do not ask for, or knowingly collect, personal information such as your name, email address or the financial figures you enter into the calculators. The only information collected automatically is standard technical data that almost all websites receive — such as your approximate location (country), browser type and pages viewed — used to keep the site secure and understand how it is used.

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use them for two purposes: to understand how the site is used (analytics) so we can improve it, and to display advertising that helps keep the calculators free to use. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertising. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising at aboutads.info/choices. If you are in the UK or the EEA, we ask for your consent before personalised ads are shown, using a Google-certified consent message.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics to count visits and see which calculators are popular. This helps us decide what to build and improve. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

Third parties we rely on

Our site is hosted on Cloudflare, and advertising is served by Google. These providers process technical data (such as IP address) as part of delivering and securing the site. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law (UK GDPR) you have rights over your personal data, including the right to access, correct or erase it, and to object to certain processing. Because we hold little or no personal data about you, most requests will simply confirm that we do not store your information. To exercise any right, or ask a question, please use our contact page.

Children

This site is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 18.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will appear on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our contact page.
